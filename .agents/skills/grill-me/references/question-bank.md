# Question Bank

Categorized questions to ask when grilling someone on a project. Pick 3-5 most relevant per conversation.

## Problem & Goals

- What's the specific problem you're solving?
- Who are the end users? (analysts, executives, engineers, customers?)
- What does "done" look like? What would make you say "this works"?
- Is this a one-time analysis or an ongoing system?
- What have you already tried?

## Scope & Constraints

- What's in scope for v1 vs. later iterations?
- What Domo features/editions do you have access to? (Pro, Enterprise, AI Service Layer, etc.)
- Any constraints on compute, budget, or timeline?
- Are there existing solutions you need to integrate with or replace?
- What's the MVP — the smallest thing that delivers value?

## Data & Integration

- What datasets are involved? Do you know their IDs?
- What's the data volume and refresh cadence?
- Are there external systems to integrate with?
- How clean is the data? Any known quality issues?
- Do you have AI Readiness / data dictionaries set up for your datasets?

## Architecture & Tech

- Are you building in App Studio, Jupyter, CodeEngine, or external?
- Do you need real-time or batch processing?
- What Domo APIs are you already familiar with?
- Does this need to run on a schedule or be triggered on demand?
- Who will maintain this after it's built?

## Governance & Security

- Who should have access to the outputs?
- Are there PDP (Personalized Data Permission) requirements?
- Any compliance constraints (SOC2, HIPAA, etc.)?
- Should the outputs be visible in Domo or exported elsewhere?
- Who approves changes to this system?

## Domo-Specific

- Have you used the AI Service Layer before? Which endpoints?
- Are you on consumption-based pricing? (AI Service Layer uses credits)
- Do you need this in a Domo App (App Studio) or standalone?
- Have you looked at existing Domo Bricks or AppStore apps that do something similar?
